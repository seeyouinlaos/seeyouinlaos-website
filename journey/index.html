The journey — the wedding of Haruthai Amphai & Suthep Thongantang.

An immersive scroll journey through our real wedding: from the Bangkok departure and the arrival in Luang Prabang to the Mekong welcome, the morning alms, the ceremony at AVANI+ Luang Prabang and the final dinner at Manda de Laos.

The departure: It begins in Bangkok.

One last golden evening on the Chao Phraya at ICONSIAM, then a short flight north into the mountains.

Sabaidee, welcome: See you in Luang Prabang.

Touch down, meet the van, and roll through the old town to the doors of AVANI+ Luang Prabang.

The welcome: Sunset on the Mekong.

From the pier we drift out for welcome drinks and dinner on the water, as the sun folds into the hills.

The morning ritual: Alms at first light.

Before the day begins, we kneel on the old-town street as the monks pass in saffron.

The wedding day: Two families, one promise.

The day gathers at the poolside in golden hour, beneath the grey Lao gables.

The celebration: Dinner among the lilies.

As dusk turns the ponds to lanternlight, we sit down to a Lao feast, cut the cake, and let the evening end quietly among the lilies.

see you in laos. — Haruthai & Suthep — 28 February 2027 · Luang Prabang, Laos
